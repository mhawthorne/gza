## Assertion inventory

- `WS-§3-PENDING-LIVE-WORKER-LIVENESS` — query/triage runtime surfaces MUST treat a `pending` task with a registered reconciled `running` worker as live, and MUST derive `stale` from worker liveness rather than the task row's empty `running_pid` alone.
